About Us

Introduction

Southern Metrology and Calibration Services, Inc. is a reputable ISO/IEC 17025:2017 accredited calibration laboratory, located at Artica Building, National Highway corner Almazora Street, Canlalay, Binan City, Laguna. Established in 2015, we have quickly become a trusted name in the industry, offering comprehensive metrology and calibration services to customers across various sectors. As the calibration laboratory in the Philippines to be accredited by the Philippine Accreditation Bureau for ISO/IEC 17025:2017, we have a track record of delivering exceptional quality and accuracy. With our customer-centric approach, state-of-the-art facilities, and a highly knowledgeable staff, we guarantee top-notch performance and professional quality services.

We specialize in providing precise and reliable metrology and calibration services tailored to the specific needs of our customers. With a wide scope of accreditation, we offer calibration services in diverse fields, including thermometry, hydrometry, mass, balances, force, pressure, torque, analytical, environmental, volume, dimensional, electrical, frequency, density, and viscosity. Our services ensure that your measurement procedures meet the highest quality standards, providing you with confidence and accuracy in your processes.
