Southern Metrology and Calibration Services, Inc. is an ISO/IEC 17025:2017 accredited calibration laboratory that offers top-notch metrology and calibration services. With over 20 years of experience, the company ensures measurement procedures are performed according to quality requirements. They provide on-site services at facilities or in state-of-the-art laboratories, with a turnaround time of 5-15 working days. They also offer logistics for collection, pick-up, and delivery services. The company is accredited by various accreditation bureaus and government agencies.
